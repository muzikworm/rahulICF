Key Points

01

Never neglect the value of a well-researched project. Look up and around for inspiration and also to catch hold of latest trends.

02

Ex

A great way to reduce workload is by working with a strategy that breaks down the stages and ensures that you keep track of all the little aspects involved. Devise a strategy from the beginning and make sure to follow it as well.

A great way to streamline work is by using applications such Trello, Figma and Asana. They allow you to set time-based progress charts which can be shared with the client as well.

03

As you proceed through the stages, engage the client as well, and make them a part of the team. Share the progress and seek feedback. It will also minimise iterations at a later stage.

04

When using external references, keep in mind the possibility of copyrights or licenses on them. The last thing you want is the work to be marked as plagiarised.
