Prevention And Cure

While a copyright is created automatically on an original work, patents have to be applied for. A patent is granted for inventions that create a product or process with industrial application.

Key Points

01

It is extremely important to apply for patent before making the work public. Prior use makes the work inapplicable from being patented.

02

Literary, dramatic, musical, artistic works or any aesthetic creation are outside the bounds of patentable items as per law. However they can be protected through a copyright. This is so since to classify as a patent, the invention needs to be a significant improvement in the existing technology, and serve a clear utility purpose.

03

A patented work can be sold or distributed only with the consent of the patent owner. Any other use will result in an infringement.

04

A patent application can be filed by anyone. However, you must be the original inventor of the work, the assignee or a legal representative. The easiest way to apply is online, or through a patent agent.

05

You can easily apply for a patent online through the www.ipindia.nic.in. After successful registration on the site, you will download the Client Software which includes all the forms to be submitted. A patent application cost is variable and can be between Rs. 30,000 to Rs. 60,000.

06

Post filling up the documents and putting a digital signature on them, you will upload the XML file online on the e-portal. A successful submission will generate an acknowledgement slip that will includes the patent application number.

07

On submission, the application goes through intensive rounds of scrutiny of the documents submitted along with the invention’s scientific relevance. A patent is granted when the application meets all these criteria.
