Trademark Your Brand

Prevention And Cure

Creating a brand that people identify with is no small effort, and protecting your brand’s identity is therefore an imperative. A trademark registration allows you to protect your identity from counterfeits and prevents misuse.

Key Points

01

Ex

Trademarks can be applied for brand name, heading, domain name, logo, and tagline, among others.

The fruit drink Frooti has a trademark on its name, as well as the tagline “Mango Frooti, Fresh and Juicy” which prevents others from using them together or individually.

02

A trademark can be filed by anyone- an individual, a company, or even a government department. It is not required for the trademark to be already in use at the time of filing.

03

A trademark application can be filed online on ipindiaonline.gov.in or at the regional Trademarks Registry Office. You can collaborate with a filing agent to take care of your application. Most of them offer great service at cheap rates, especially via online platforms.

04

Your first step to trademark registration involves looking up for names on http://ipindiaonline.gov.in/tmrpublicsearch/frmmain.aspx to see if any match yours. If there are no exact or similar matches, then you are good to go to the next step which involves selecting your product category from a pre-defined list.

05

The formal procedure begins with filling the online form, which comes with a one-time fee of Rs. 4,000 and submission of supporting documents. On successful submission, you are allotted a trademark application number. You can start using the ™ symbol with the receipt of this number itself.

06

Following the submission, the application is reviewed by the Trademarks Officer, and any objections are notified to you. You have the right to reply to these objections and support your claim.

07

Once satisfied, the application is opened up to external objections, post which the registration certificate is granted. You can start using the ® symbol!

08

From submission till the final issue of the registration certificate, the process takes about 15 to 18 months. However, you can start using the ™ symbol within 24 hours of successful acknowledgement of your application number.

09

The trademark filing process takes some time, but goes a long way in building a unique brand recognition, and prevents others to take advantage of your work.
